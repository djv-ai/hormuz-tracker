STRAIT OF HORMUZ · MARITIME INTELLIGENCE SYSTEM · v3

WHAT IS THIS TRACKER?

This tool monitors maritime vessel traffic through the Strait of Hormuz — one of the world's most strategically critical waterways.

It tracks visible vessel movements, dark transits, traffic flow, and disruptions across the Persian Gulf, the Strait of Hormuz, and the Gulf of Oman.

VESSEL TYPES

VESSEL COLOURS & TYPES

Cyan — Tanker / LNG Carrier

Green — Cargo / Container

Red — Naval / Military

Amber — Unknown

Orange — Currently dark (AIS off)

DARK TRANSITS

A vessel may appear in one basin, go dark near the strait, then reappear in the other basin. Reappeared vessels are highlighted and their route is shown in the detail panel.
